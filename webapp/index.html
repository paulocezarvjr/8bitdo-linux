Remap your keyboard.
No Windows. No installs.

A browser configurator for the 8BitDo Retro Mechanical Keyboard, talking straight to the hardware over WebHID. Click a key, press what you want it to become. Done.

  • Plug in by USB cable, power switch OFF
  • Use Chrome or Edge (WebHID)
  • Press the 8BitDo key on the board to make remaps live
